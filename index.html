Nepali Web App

Techno sewa is a nepali web app for all kind of home service at doorstep. It is a digital platform. The idea generated from the realization by the trio regarding the lack of proper day to day services required by homes and corporate offices in Kathmandu and the hassle one had to face for even the smallest of problems related to plumbing or electricity. Now after 5 years of successful operations and growth during these years the team have realized the potential for expansion and have re-engineered their operations and rebranded themselves as TECHNO SEWA.
